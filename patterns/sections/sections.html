Loyal Rescue

Loyal Rescue Inc. is a foster-based rescue and we do not

operate through a shelter. Loyal Rescue was established

in 2005 as an Ontario-based rescue who strongly believes

that shelter dogs deserve better than being abandoned by

those who are tasked with being responsible for them.
